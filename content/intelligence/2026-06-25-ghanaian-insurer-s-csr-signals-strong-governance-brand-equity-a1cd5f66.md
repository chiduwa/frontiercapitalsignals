Imperial General Assurance, a Ghanaian insurer, through its "Imperial Girl" CSR initiative, partnered with World Vision to distribute menstrual hygiene materials to seven schools in Ghana's Eastern Region. This intervention directly addresses period poverty, empowering young girls in rural communities.

This move by Imperial General Assurance signals a strategic commitment to strong corporate social responsibility and community engagement. For investors in financial services, robust CSR enhances brand reputation, customer loyalty, and secures a valuable social license to operate in Ghana. It indicates a management focused on sustainable practices, potentially mitigating long-term social risks.
