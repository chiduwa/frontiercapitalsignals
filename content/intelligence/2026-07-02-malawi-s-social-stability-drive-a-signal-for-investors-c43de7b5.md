Malawi's Department of Civic Education has launched an awareness campaign in Mzimba District to combat violence and false accusations of witchcraft against elderly people. This initiative seeks to alleviate fear and improve safety for older persons within their communities.

While seemingly a social issue, such campaigns are vital for investor confidence. Addressing witchcraft violence signifies a government's commitment to rule of law, protecting citizens, and fostering social stability. A predictable, secure societal environment reduces operational risks for businesses, enhancing long-term investment attractiveness in the region.

Monitor the campaign's effectiveness in reducing reported incidents and improving community safety. Observe if similar initiatives are implemented in other districts or if this reflects a broader governmental push to strengthen local governance and human rights protections. Broader success in these areas would signal a more stable and attractive investment climate.
