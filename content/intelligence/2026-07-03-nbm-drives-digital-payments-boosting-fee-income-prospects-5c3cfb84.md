National Bank of Malawi (NBM) plc recently completed the third draw of its ‘Waku WAFCON’ POS Promotion, rewarding 20 customers with official Malawi National Women’s Football Team jerseys. This campaign incentivized point-of-sale transactions by engaging customers in a 'Guess the Scorchers WAFCON Jersey' activity.

This promotion signals NBM's aggressive push to deepen digital payment adoption across Malawi. Increased POS usage directly translates to higher transaction fee revenue for the bank and indicates a growing consumer comfort with electronic payments. Such initiatives are key for financial institutions aiming to capture market share in a digitizing economy.

Investors should monitor NBM's upcoming financial reports for indicators of increased transaction volumes and non-interest income from digital channels. We also advise observing competitor responses and broader regulatory support for digital payments infrastructure in Malawi, as these factors will influence the sustained growth of this revenue stream.
