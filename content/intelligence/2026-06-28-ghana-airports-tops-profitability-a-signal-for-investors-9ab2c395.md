Ghana Airports Company Limited (GACL) was recognized as the ‘Most Profitable State-Owned Enterprise’ for 2022 at the Public Enterprises League Table Awards. This commendation from the State Interests and Governance Authority highlights GACL's exceptional financial performance and efficiency within the public sector.

This achievement signals robust operational health and effective management within a critical national infrastructure asset. For investors, GACL's profitability demonstrates the potential for consistent returns and sustainable growth in Ghana's aviation sector. It suggests a conducive environment for well-run enterprises, potentially opening doors for future private sector involvement or expansion opportunities.
