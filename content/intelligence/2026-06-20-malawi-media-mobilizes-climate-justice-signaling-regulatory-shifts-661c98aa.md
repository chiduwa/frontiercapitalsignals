ActionAid Malawi is actively mobilizing the local media to amplify its "Fund Our Future Climate Justice Campaign." The initiative calls for journalists to expose climate injustice and pressure major polluters, aiming to build significant public momentum for policy changes.

For investors, this signals a potential rise in environmental, social, and governance (ESG) scrutiny within Malawi. Increased media attention can lead to greater public pressure, potentially translating into new regulatory demands or stricter enforcement affecting industries perceived as environmentally intensive, such as mining, large-scale agriculture, and traditional energy. This also highlights opportunities in climate resilience and sustainable solutions.

We recommend monitoring the media landscape for sustained coverage and the campaign's traction among policymakers and the public. Watch for any governmental responses, including discussions around environmental legislation, new taxation mechanisms for polluters, or specific industry directives. Assess corporate reactions and their commitment to climate adaptation strategies.
