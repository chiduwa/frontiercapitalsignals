Africa's major development projects are undergoing a significant strategic pivot. The Africa Report's selection of 20 'Big Builds' highlights a continent-wide shift towards prioritizing local value creation rather than just large-scale infrastructure. This signals a new era for regional development initiatives.

For investors, this reorientation creates distinct opportunities. Capital deployment can now target sectors supporting domestic industrialization, processing, and manufacturing, moving beyond raw resource extraction. While ambitious and potentially uncertain, this focus on homegrown value chains offers pathways to more resilient, integrated economic growth.

Next, closely watch specific project details, national policy frameworks, and government commitment to fostering these value creation initiatives. Identify industries poised to benefit most from this localized approach. Monitor execution progress and financing models to gauge the viability and potential returns of these emerging opportunities.
