Providus Bank and Unity Bank have completed their merger, commencing operations as a unified institution, ProvidusUnity Bank. This move combines their strengths, aiming to create a more robust platform for customers and the broader Nigerian economy.

For investors, this signals significant consolidation within Nigeria's banking sector. The new entity brings a larger balance sheet and potentially expanded reach, which could improve efficiency and capitalize on synergies. This development creates a new contender, influencing market dynamics and potentially driving further M&A activity among smaller or mid-tier banks.
