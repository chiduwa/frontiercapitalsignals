Press Properties Limited, PPL, has commenced construction of the five-storey TNM Office Park in Blantyre, Malawi. This significant commercial development, located on Plot BC1097, will be anchored by TNM Plc and is slated for completion within 36 months.

This project signals strong, long-term demand for premium commercial real estate in Blantyre, Malawi's economic hub. It underscores local confidence in the country’s growth trajectory and presents opportunities across construction, related services, and the broader urban development sector.

Investors should closely track construction milestones and future tenant occupancy rates to assess market absorption and project returns. Further, monitor any subsequent infrastructure upgrades and policy support for foreign investment in Malawi's evolving real estate landscape.
