Eight leading Nigerian banks achieved an average revenue of N332.74 million per employee, as reported for 2025. Access Bank and Zenith Bank led this impressive performance, driven primarily by significant, aggressive investments in technology across their operations.

This metric signals robust operational efficiency and productivity gains within Nigeria's banking sector. For investors, high revenue per employee indicates effective resource deployment, strong management, and scalability, suggesting a favorable environment for sustained profitability and attractive returns.
