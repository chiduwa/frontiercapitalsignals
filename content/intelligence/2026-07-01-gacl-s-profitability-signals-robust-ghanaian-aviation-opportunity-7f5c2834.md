Ghana Airports Company Limited (GACL) earned the prestigious title of 'Most Profitable State-Owned Enterprise' for 2022 at the Public Enterprises League Table (PELT) Awards. This recognition, awarded by SIGA, specifically commended GACL for achieving the highest profitability parameters among its peers.

This award signals GACL's strong operational efficiency and robust financial health within Ghana's critical aviation infrastructure. Consistent profitability for a key state asset indicates stability, potential for capital reinvestment, and a well-managed entity. It may also suggest future opportunities for public-private partnerships or privatization, aligning with broader government initiatives to improve SOE performance.

Monitor any indications of GACL's expansion projects, updated dividend policies, or government plans regarding further private sector participation in the aviation sector. Observing the overall trajectory of Ghana's aviation traffic and tourist arrivals will also be crucial for gauging sustained profitability.
