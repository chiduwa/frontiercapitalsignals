Africa's ultra-high-net-worth (UHNW) population surged 24%, hitting 3,440 individuals with combined wealth of $400 billion. This marks the fastest UHNW growth globally, underscoring significant wealth creation on the continent.

This surge, driven by improved financing, stronger currencies, and critical mineral investments, signals a burgeoning domestic capital pool. Investors should recognize the increasing demand for high-end services, sophisticated financial products, and direct investment opportunities from a rapidly expanding local investor class.

Watch for the projected UHNW population growth to 5,200 by 2025, a 51% increase, and how this wealth deploys into key sectors. Focus on opportunities in private credit, real estate, and sophisticated financial instruments. Monitor regulatory frameworks evolving to support or attract this growing pool of indigenous capital across our focus markets.
