The 2026 FIFA World Cup has integrated significant technology. AI now plays a key role in officiating and match analysis, while the official match ball includes an embedded microchip.

This signals a global acceleration in sports technology. For African markets like Ghana, this creates demand for local tech talent, data infrastructure, and specialized software development, potentially attracting foreign direct investment into these high-growth areas.

Monitor government initiatives supporting sports tech ecosystems and private sector partnerships with global sports innovation leaders. Watch for increased startup activity in AI-driven analytics and digital sports platforms across the region, particularly in Ghana.
