The digital economy is transitioning from institutional data control to consumer data ownership, driven by open banking and Web3 principles. This paradigm shift fundamentally redefines how value is created and exchanged.

This evolution unlocks immense potential for financial innovation and inclusion in markets like Nigeria, Kenya, and Ghana. Investors should identify agile fintechs leveraging open APIs and Web3 solutions for new data-driven services. Traditional institutions failing to adapt risk losing competitive ground as consumers demand more control and better-tailored offerings.

Monitor the pace of regulatory adoption for data privacy and open banking frameworks. Watch for emerging Web3 financial infrastructure and strategic partnerships that accelerate the monetization of consumer-controlled data assets.
