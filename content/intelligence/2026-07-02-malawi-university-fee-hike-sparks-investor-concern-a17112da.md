MAPUSU, the Malawi Public Universities Students’ Union, is set to engage the government regarding a 100% tuition fee increase implemented by public universities. Student leaders have formally expressed serious concern over these significant hikes.

This situation matters for investors as substantial tuition increases frequently trigger social unrest, including protests, which can undermine stability and investor confidence. It also signals potential fiscal challenges for the government, influencing its policy decisions and resource allocation.

We advise watching the outcome of MAPUSU's negotiations with the government closely. Monitor for any government concessions or sustained student protests. Assess the broader public reaction and the potential for wider social or political ramifications that could impact local market stability and investment climate.
