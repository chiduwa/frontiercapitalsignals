Malawi's Chitipa First Grade Magistrate Court convicted George Mulungu for illegally cultivating and possessing 84.6 kilograms of *Cannabis sativa*. He received a K1 million fine after being found guilty on July 2.

This incident highlights Malawi's ongoing strict enforcement against unauthorized cannabis operations, despite recent discussions around potential liberalization for medical or industrial purposes. Investors exploring agricultural opportunities, especially in novel crops, must remain acutely aware of the existing legal framework and regulatory risks.

Monitor legislative developments closely, particularly any official moves towards establishing a regulated medical or industrial hemp sector. Continued enforcement trends and the government's stance on attracting legitimate agricultural investment in alternative crops will be key indicators for future market entry.
