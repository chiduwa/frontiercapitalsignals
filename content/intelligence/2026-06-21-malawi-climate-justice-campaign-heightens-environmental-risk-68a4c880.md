ActionAid Malawi is escalating its 'Fund Our Future Climate Justice Campaign' by calling on local journalists to actively expose climate injustice and pressure major polluters. This move aims to build significant public and political momentum behind environmental accountability.

This initiative signals a growing focus on environmental accountability in Malawi. Investors in sectors with significant environmental footprints, such as agriculture, mining, or manufacturing, should anticipate increased scrutiny. This could translate into new regulatory pressures, demands for compensation, or heightened operational costs for perceived 'big polluters'.

Monitor the Malawian media landscape for specific companies or industries targeted by this campaign. Observe government's response, potential policy shifts related to environmental compliance, and any emergence of new climate-related taxes or funds designed to address climate injustice.
