HMD and its Tuboshu brand unveiled ForLife, a lifetime equipment protection scheme, at WAMPEX 2026 in Accra, Ghana. This program guarantees coverage against the full lifespan cost of heavy machinery breakdowns.

This initiative significantly de-risks operational expenditures for companies in the mining and power sectors. ForLife offers enhanced cost predictability and improves the long-term viability of capital-intensive projects, making these investments more attractive to international capital.

Investors should monitor the adoption rate of ForLife among heavy equipment operators in Ghana and across West Africa. Observe the tangible impact on operational efficiencies, project profitability, and any competitive responses in the equipment service market.
