Malawi's national women's football team, the Scorchers, has departed for Morocco to make its historic debut at the 2026 Women's Africa Cup of Nations, WAFCON. Head Coach Lovemore Fazili expressed strong confidence in his squad as they departed Kamuzu International Airport.

This event is more than just a sporting achievement. Malawi's presence on such a significant continental stage enhances national visibility and soft power. For investors, this increased international profile can subtly boost country branding, potentially stimulating tourism interest and driving domestic consumer spending related to sports, media, and merchandise. It signals a nation capable of achieving international recognition.
