Flutterwave, in collaboration with Xoom, a PayPal service, has launched direct international money transfers to Nigerian bank accounts. This integration connects Xoom’s global network with Flutterwave’s established local payout infrastructure in Nigeria.

This partnership significantly streamlines remittances into Nigeria, one of Africa's largest and fastest-growing digital economies. It enhances financial inclusion and efficiency for senders and recipients, cementing Flutterwave's position as a critical enabler of cross-border payments in a high-volume market. For investors, this validates the continued expansion and robustness of Nigeria's digital financial services sector.
