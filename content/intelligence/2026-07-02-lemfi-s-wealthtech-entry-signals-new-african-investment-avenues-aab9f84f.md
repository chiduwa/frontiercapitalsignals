LemFi, a leading financial platform for people living and working across borders, has secured UK FCA approval for its acquisition of investment platform Wealth8. This strategic move marks its entry into wealth management and investment services, significantly expanding its product ecosystem.

This acquisition is significant for investors as it diversifies LemFi's revenue streams beyond remittances, enhancing its customer lifetime value within the African diaspora. It positions the company to capitalize on the growing demand for accessible investment solutions, driving innovation and potentially fostering new partnerships or acquisition targets in the wealthtech space.
