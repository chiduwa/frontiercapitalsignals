A Deputy Minister in Malawi allegedly tried to oust his own line Minister, making secret recommendations to the appointing authority. This extraordinary move, confirmed by government sources, has created significant internal shockwaves within Cabinet.

Such blatant internal power struggles signal heightened political instability and potential governance dysfunction. This reduces policy predictability and introduces unwarranted political risk, which can deter foreign direct investment and complicate long-term business planning.

Watch for the President's response and any official disciplinary actions against the Deputy Minister. Monitor the broader fallout within Cabinet and its potential impact on policy execution, particularly for key economic portfolios. Any prolonged infighting could further erode investor confidence.
