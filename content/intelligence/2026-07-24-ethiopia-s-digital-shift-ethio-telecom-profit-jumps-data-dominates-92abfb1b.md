Ethio Telecom reported a significant jump in annual earnings, driven by a historic shift in revenue. Mobile data and internet services have now overtaken traditional voice calls as the operator's primary income source, signaling a rapid acceleration in Ethiopia's digital adoption.

This shift signals a crucial trend for investors: the immense, scalable potential of data-driven services across frontier markets. It validates significant investment into digital infrastructure, network expansion, and innovative service offerings, promising higher-margin revenue growth compared to legacy voice services.

Investors should monitor the sustainability of data revenue growth, especially ARPU trends, and the pace of further digital service integration. Key indicators include continued infrastructure investment, regulatory shifts, and the competitive landscape, particularly how operators leverage digital adoption for fintech and enterprise solutions.
