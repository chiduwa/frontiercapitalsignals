Ukraine's expanded drone campaign is directly targeting Russian refineries and export infrastructure. This disrupts global diesel supplies and wheat shipments, raising immediate supply concerns for key commodities.

For our focus markets in Ghana, Nigeria, Kenya, Malawi, and Uganda, this translates directly to increased fuel and food inflation. Businesses face higher input costs, while consumers see reduced discretionary spending, impacting sectors from logistics to retail and agriculture.

Monitor global crude and wheat price trends closely, alongside local government responses to mitigate inflationary pressures. Watch for any accelerated moves towards energy and food import diversification, potentially presenting new investment avenues.
