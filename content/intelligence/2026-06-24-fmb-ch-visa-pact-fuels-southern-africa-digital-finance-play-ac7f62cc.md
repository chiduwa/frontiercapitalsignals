FMB Capital Holdings, the parent company for First Capital Bank, sealed a five-year strategic deal with Visa. This partnership aims to accelerate digital payments and advance financial inclusion across Southern Africa, unifying FMB CH subsidiaries in Malawi, Botswana, Mozambique, Zambia, and Zimbabwe under a single framework.

This collaboration is a significant catalyst for digital payment adoption and financial inclusion across five key African markets. Visa’s global expertise and resources validate FMB CH’s regional strategy, enhancing its capacity to tap into large unbanked populations. This move positions First Capital Bank for increased transaction volume growth and expanded market share in the rapidly evolving digital finance sector.

Investors should monitor the speed and scale of implementation, especially user adoption rates and growth in digital transaction volumes across FMB CH’s operating countries. Watch for competitive responses from other regional banks and payment providers, and track any supportive regulatory shifts that could further accelerate digital payment infrastructure development in the region.
