People's Pension Trust (PPT), in collaboration with MTN Mobile Money Fintech Limited, has launched FlexiPension in Ghana. This new digital personal pension product aims to extend retirement coverage to millions of informal sector workers, leveraging mobile money infrastructure for wider reach.

This initiative represents a significant opportunity in Ghana's financial sector. Tapping into the vast, underserved informal economy promises substantial asset under management growth for pension providers and new revenue streams for mobile money platforms. It deepens financial inclusion, potentially boosting long-term domestic savings and capital market development.

Monitor FlexiPension's subscriber uptake and contribution rates closely. We also need to watch for regulatory support or competitive responses, as this model could prove highly scalable across other African markets with large informal sectors and strong mobile money penetration.
