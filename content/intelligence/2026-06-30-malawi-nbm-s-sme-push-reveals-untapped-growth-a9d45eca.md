National Bank of Malawi (NBM) plc hosted a significant SME Day Exhibition in Mzuzu, bringing together 50 businesses, customers, and the public. This event, commemorating World MSME Day, underscored the bank's sustained commitment to supporting micro, small, and medium enterprises.

This active engagement from a major financial institution like NBM indicates increasing formalization and professionalization within Malawi's SME sector. Bank-led initiatives like this not only provide crucial visibility and market access for these businesses but also signal a growing, investable asset class. It suggests improved risk profiles and potential for scale.
