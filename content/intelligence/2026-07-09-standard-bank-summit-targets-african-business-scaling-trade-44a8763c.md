Standard Bank hosted its "Africa Unlocked 2026" conference in Cape Town, bringing together over 140 top African business leaders, policymakers, and global investors. The urgent focus was how to scale African businesses and unlock intra-continental trade amidst an increasingly fragmented global economy.

This high-level gathering underscores a significant strategic intent to deepen regional economic integration and foster indigenous business growth across Africa. Increased intra-continental trade reduces reliance on external markets, potentially stabilizing supply chains and creating new demand centers, which is favorable for regional equities and cross-border ventures.

Monitor for concrete policy recommendations, investment commitments, or new cross-border initiatives emerging from this conference. Specifically, look for partnerships between participating business leaders and any regulatory shifts designed to ease trade barriers in the coming months, particularly within major regional blocs.
