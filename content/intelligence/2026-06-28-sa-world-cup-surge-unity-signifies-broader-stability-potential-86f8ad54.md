While a sports story, such national achievements, particularly when founded on unity and strong internal dynamics, can project a powerful image beyond the field. For investors, this soft power translates into improved national brand equity, potentially fostering a perception of stability and cohesive societal functioning. This can subtly enhance a country's attractiveness for foreign direct investment.
