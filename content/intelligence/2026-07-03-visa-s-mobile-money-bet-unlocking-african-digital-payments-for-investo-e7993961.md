Visa, through Aminata Kane Ndiaye, its head for West and Central Africa, is aggressively pursuing digital payments expansion across the continent. Her core strategy involves a significant bet on mobile money, leveraging her deep connections within telecoms and startup ecosystems.

This signals a major global player's commitment to Africa's mobile money growth story. Investors should note the significant upside for fintech enablers, telecom infrastructure providers, and digital consumer platforms. This strategy validates the continent's digital transformation, offering compelling entry points across the financial services value chain.

Watch for Visa's upcoming partnerships with major mobile money operators and fintech startups, particularly in high-volume markets. Monitor new product rollouts and regulatory shifts supporting digital payment interoperability and cross-border transactions. These will dictate the pace of Visa's ecosystem integration and market penetration.
