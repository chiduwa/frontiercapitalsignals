Mohammed Dewji’s MeTL Group is making a $275 million move into graphite mining in Tanzania. Commercial production for this significant project is expected to begin within 18 months. This investment places a major East African conglomerate directly into the global competition for critical battery materials.

This deployment of $275 million in private capital signals growing confidence in Tanzania's mining sector and its strategic position in the global electric vehicle supply chain. It underscores East Africa's potential to become a significant source of critical minerals, attracting further investment interest in the region's resource plays.

Investors should monitor the project's execution timeline and progress toward the 18-month commercial production target. Watch for any indications of MeTL's plans for local battery processing, which remains the larger prize. Also, track broader Tanzanian government policies on mineral value addition and export.
