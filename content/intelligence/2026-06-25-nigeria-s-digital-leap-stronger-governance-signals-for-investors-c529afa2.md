Nigeria's National Agency for Science and Engineering Infrastructure (NASENI) and the Code of Conduct Bureau (CCB) have partnered to digitize asset declarations for five million public servants. This initiative aims to streamline the process and improve data integrity for compliance.

This digital transformation represents a significant step towards enhanced transparency and accountability within Nigeria's public sector. For investors, improved governance frameworks and reduced corruption risks translate to a more predictable and stable operating environment. It also signals potential for further tech-driven solutions in government services.

Monitor the implementation timeline and effectiveness of this new digital system, particularly any associated tenders or technology contracts. Also, observe its impact on Nigeria's standing in global corruption perception indices and overall ease of doing business metrics. Successful deployment could pave the way for broader public sector digitization.
