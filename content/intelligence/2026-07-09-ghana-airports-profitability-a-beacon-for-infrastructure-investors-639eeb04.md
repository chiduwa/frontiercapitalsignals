Ghana Airports Company Limited (GACL) was named the "Most Profitable State-Owned Enterprise" for 2022 at the Public Enterprises League Table Awards, an event hosted by the State Interests and Governance Authority (SIGA). This prestigious recognition highlights GACL's superior financial performance and operational success.

This award signals robust demand and efficient management within Ghana's aviation sector. For investors, it indicates a stable and profitable environment for infrastructure-related investments, including potential public-private partnerships or debt instruments linked to well-performing state assets. It reinforces confidence in Ghana's economic infrastructure.

Monitor GACL's future financial disclosures for sustained profitability and growth. Watch for government policy announcements concerning airport expansion projects, infrastructure upgrades, or potential privatization strategies. Additionally, assess broader trends in Ghana's travel and tourism sector, which directly impacts GACL's revenue streams.
