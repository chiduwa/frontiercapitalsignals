Ghana Airports Company Limited (GACL) secured the 'Most Profitable State-Owned Enterprise' title for 2022 at the Public Enterprises League Table (PELT) Awards. This recognition, from the State Interests and Governance Authority (SIGA), specifically commended GACL for achieving the highest profitability parameter among state-owned enterprises.

This signifies strong operational performance and financial health within a critical infrastructure sector in Ghana. For investors eyeing Ghanaian assets, GACL's consistent profitability points to a well-managed entity with robust revenue streams, likely driven by increasing air traffic and efficient cost management. It indicates potential for stable returns in a generally challenging SOE landscape.

Monitor GACL's upcoming financial reports for 2023 and beyond to assess sustained profitability and growth. Look for any strategic expansion plans, private sector partnerships, or government divestiture intentions that could open direct investment avenues. Also, track overall air traffic recovery and airport development projects in Ghana.
