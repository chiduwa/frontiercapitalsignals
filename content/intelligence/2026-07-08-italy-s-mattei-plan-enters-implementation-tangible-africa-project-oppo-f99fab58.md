Italy's flagship Mattei Plan has transitioned from diplomatic commitments to active project implementation across Africa. Initial focus areas include transport, energy, agriculture, and digital infrastructure. This marks a significant shift from planning to tangible execution.

For investors, this signals immediate, concrete opportunities. We anticipate increased capital flows and forthcoming tenders in critical sectors. African nations are prioritizing investment, job creation, and industrial growth, directly aligning with private sector development goals.

Our focus shifts to specific project announcements and tender releases, identifying which countries and sectors receive the initial tranches of investment. Watch for key partnerships between Italian and local firms, which will highlight clear market entry points and early-stage growth plays.
