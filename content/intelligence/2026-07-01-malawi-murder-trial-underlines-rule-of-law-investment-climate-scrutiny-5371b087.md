The High Court’s Criminal Division in Lilongwe resumes the murder trial of Memory Kamange. Suspect Phillip Malenga is expected to be paraded, with the court hearing state testimony and conducting a crime scene visit as prosecution proceeds.

While a specific criminal case, such high-profile trials offer a lens into Malawi's broader rule of law environment and judicial efficiency. A robust, transparent legal system is fundamental for investor confidence, safeguarding assets, and ensuring predictable business operations.

Monitor the trial's progression, particularly the efficiency and transparency of court proceedings and the final judgment. These factors will continue to shape perceptions of Malawi's judicial integrity and its commitment to a stable, secure investment climate.
