EthSwitch, Ethiopia's national payments switch, posted a ETB 2.6 billion gross profit before tax for FY 2025/26. The company processed 387 million interoperable transactions valued at ETB 1.26 trillion, indicating surging digital payments adoption within Africa's second most populous nation.

This strong performance underscores Ethiopia's rapid digital transformation within its financial sector. The massive transaction volume and profit reveal a fertile ground for fintech innovation, financial infrastructure development, and tech-driven payment solutions, appealing to investors seeking scalable growth in Africa's major economies.

Monitor regulatory developments impacting foreign investment in Ethiopia's financial services and tech sectors. Watch for further expansion of EthSwitch's network, new payment integrations, and emerging private sector players capitalizing on this accelerating digital shift.
