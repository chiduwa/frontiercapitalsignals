Kaduna State Governor Uba Sani recently flagged off 11 road projects in Zaria. He publicly linked this ongoing infrastructure development to President Tinubu's consistent support for the state, pledging 98 percent of Kaduna's votes for Tinubu in 2027.

This explicit political alignment between a key northern state and the federal administration is a clear signal for investors. It suggests Kaduna will remain a priority for federal investment and support. For infrastructure investors, this implies a potentially lower political risk profile, smoother project execution, and sustained commitment to development within such aligned states.

Watch for the actual execution speed of these road projects and any further federal financial commitments to Kaduna. Also, observe how other state governments position themselves politically to secure similar federal backing for their own development agendas.
