Market dynamics consistently show many investors equate price with intrinsic value in commercial real estate, overlooking crucial underlying factors that drive long-term returns.

For investors targeting Ghana, Nigeria, Kenya, Malawi, and Uganda, this common oversight can lead to suboptimal returns or missed opportunities. True value in these dynamic markets stems from factors like strategic location, quality tenancy, lease structures, and demonstrable growth potential, not merely the asking price. Sophisticated valuation is paramount.

Moving forward, prioritize rigorous due diligence and local market expertise beyond headline prices. Focus on identifying assets where current pricing undervalues strong underlying fundamentals, stable income streams, and long-term capital appreciation potential.
