Pepkor Holdings has merged its fintech subsidiary, Flash, with Shop2Shop, creating FintechCo, now one of South Africa's largest merchant commerce and fintech platforms. The combined entity handles over R200 billion in annual transactions, with Pepkor securing a 57.1% controlling stake.

This R21.3 billion deal highlights the immense value and scalability within Africa's informal economy fintech sector. It showcases the significant investment potential in digital solutions for SMEs and informal traders, a critical growth segment across the continent, including our focus markets. The consolidation reflects growing maturity and value creation within African fintech.
