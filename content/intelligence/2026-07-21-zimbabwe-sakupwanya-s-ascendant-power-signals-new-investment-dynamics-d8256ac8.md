Pedzai ‘Scott’ Sakupwanya, a prominent Zimbabwean gold baron, is rapidly consolidating power and influence. A ZANU-PF financier, he has transitioned into politics, while maintaining significant interests across mining, energy, and logistics sectors.

Sakupwanya's deep political connections and control over critical sectors signal concentrated economic power within Zimbabwe. Investors should note that accessing opportunities, especially in mining, energy, or logistics, may increasingly require navigating his sphere of influence or aligning with entities linked to him.

Monitor Sakupwanya's legislative actions, public statements, and any further acquisitions or divestments in his core areas. His trajectory will serve as a bellwether for political-economic trends and potential state-backed favoritism in Zimbabwe's key industries.
