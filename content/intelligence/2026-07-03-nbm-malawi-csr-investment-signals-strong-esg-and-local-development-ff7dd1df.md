National Bank of Malawi (NBM) plc recently completed a solar-powered water reticulation system for Mikate Health Post in Thyolo. This initiative forms part of the bank's K200 million Corporate Social Responsibility program, directly supporting healthcare service delivery and community development.

This move signals NBM's commitment to long-term sustainability and local community development. For investors, this reflects a financially stable and socially responsible entity, crucial for mitigating ESG risks and attracting impact-focused capital. It also highlights the growing integration of solar solutions in essential services, indicating a viable trend for ancillary investment opportunities in clean energy and rural infrastructure.
