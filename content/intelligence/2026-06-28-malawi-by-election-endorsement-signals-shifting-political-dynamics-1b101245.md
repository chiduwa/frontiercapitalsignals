Malawi's Second Vice President, Enock Chihana, publicly endorsed Democratic Progressive Party (DPP) candidate Mike Chinzukira for the Lilongwe City Bwaila by-election. This endorsement, made at the final campaign event, aims to boost DPP's momentum ahead of the June 30 vote.

This signals potential shifts in political alliances and strengthens the opposition DPP's visibility. A stronger, more cohesive opposition could lead to greater policy scrutiny and potentially impact investor confidence in long-term regulatory predictability, especially concerning major infrastructure or development projects linked to such political endorsements.
