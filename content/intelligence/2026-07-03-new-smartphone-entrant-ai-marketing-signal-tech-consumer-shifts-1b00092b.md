Packard Bell has entered the smartphone market with its Daytona G16 Pro, an Android device aiming for premium features at an accessible price point. This launch is notable for also featuring a fully AI-generated advertising campaign, from concept to script.

This move signals increasing competition in the affordable premium smartphone segment, a rapidly expanding market across Africa. For investors, it indicates potential shifts in consumer electronics market share and increased demand for digital advertising solutions. The AI-driven ad campaign highlights a growing trend in marketing efficiency and technology adoption, potentially impacting traditional advertising agencies and boosting AI service providers.
