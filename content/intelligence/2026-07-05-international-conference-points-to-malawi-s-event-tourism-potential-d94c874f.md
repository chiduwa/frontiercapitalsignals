A significant international Christian conference is currently underway in Blantyre, Malawi, headlined by a prominent Nigerian apostle making his first visit. The Healing Miracle Revival Conference, organized by Raised for a Purpose Ministry, is drawing participants from Australia, Nigeria, and the broader region.

This signals a notable inflow of international visitors and domestic movement into Blantyre, boosting local hospitality, transport, and retail sectors. Such large-scale religious events generate significant short-term economic activity, demonstrating Blantyre's capacity to host major gatherings and manage demand surges in its service economy.
